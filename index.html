About

A trader who
teaches honestly

I am Debpratim Ghosh — a stock market trader and educator based in West Bengal, India. I trade equities for a living and teach because I believe financial literacy should be accessible to everyone, regardless of their background.

My channel and courses are built for the absolute beginner — someone who has never opened a trading terminal, never heard of a candlestick, and doesn't know the difference between Sensex and Nifty. I was that person once too.

Every video I create is designed to be clear, jargon-free, and practical — rooted in real market understanding, not theory for its own sake.

📈
Active Trader First
I trade the Indian markets daily. What I teach comes from real experience, not just textbooks.
🎓
Beginner Obsessed
Every concept is explained from zero. No prior knowledge assumed, ever.
🇮🇳
India-Focused
NSE, BSE, Sensex, Nifty — everything is taught in the Indian market context.
